Meet Mindi

Your 24/7 AI wellness
companion

Mindi is powered by Claude AI and trained to be a culturally respectful, compassionate listener. She never diagnoses — she listens, guides, and supports.

Emotionally Intelligent

Mindi recognises distress and responds with warmth, offering breathing exercises and grounding techniques tailored to your situation.

Culturally Aware

Designed specifically for Ethiopian contexts — respectful of local values, language nuances, and community dynamics.

Privacy-First

All conversations are secured. Mindi gently encourages professional help when appropriate without sharing your data.

Our story

Built in Ethiopia,
for Ethiopians

WellMind Ethiopia was built at a wellness hackathon by a passionate team who believe mental health support should be accessible, culturally respectful, and available to everyone — regardless of background.

🏆

Wellness Hackathon 2025

Built in 48 hours with love and ambition

Accessibility First

Mental wellness tools should be available to everyone — not just those who can afford therapy sessions.

Cultural Respect

We understand Ethiopian values, community dynamics, and the nuances of mental health conversations in our context.

Privacy & Safety

Your data is yours. We use secure JWT auth, never sell data, and Mindi always recommends professional help when needed.
